Privacy Policy for GoingDark Extension

Last Updated: March 2024

Data Collection

The GoingDark extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data. No data is sent to any servers.

  • No personal information collected
  • No browsing history tracked
  • No user data stored
  • No data transmitted to external servers
  • No cookies or analytics used

Permissions Explanation

"activeTab" permission: This permission allows the extension to only access the currently active browser tab when you click the extension icon. It cannot access other tabs, browsing history, or browser data.

How It Works

GoingDark applies visual CSS filters and overlays locally in your browser. All processing happens on your device. No data leaves your computer.
